Frequently asked questions

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 1–2 business days and arrive within 4–8 business days depending on destination.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes — orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping.

What is your return policy?

Unused items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. See the returns page for details.

How can I contact support?

You can use the contact page to reach support for orders, product questions, or return requests.

Are these products curated?

Yes — the assortment is intentionally compact and selected for everyday utility, giftability, and trend appeal.
